March 10, 1997

Netscape vs Internet Explorer - The Browser Wars!

Fellow web surfers, we're living in historic times! The battle for browser supremacy is heating up and I have some hot takes to share.

Netscape Navigator: The original and still the best! Great features, reliable, and it respects web standards. Plus, it has that cool "N" logo that pulses when a page is loading.

Internet Explorer: Microsoft's answer to Netscape. It's... okay, I guess? It comes with Windows 95, which is convenient, but it feels like they're trying to control the whole internet.

My prediction? Netscape will always be the king of browsers. Microsoft should stick to operating systems and leave the web to the experts!

What browser are you using? I'm curious to hear from my readers!

March 5, 1997

Building My First Website - Tips and Tricks

Hey everyone! After weeks of learning HTML by hand-coding in Notepad, I finally launched my personal homepage. Here are some tips for fellow aspiring webmasters:

Essential Elements Every Site Needs:

  • A visitor counter (how else will people know you're popular?)
  • Animated GIFs - the more, the better!
  • A guestbook for visitor feedback
  • Links to your favorite sites
  • Under construction signs (very important!)

Remember: if your site doesn't have at least 3 different font colors and a blinking element, you're doing it wrong! üòÑ

The web is all about expressing yourself and having fun. Don't worry about looking "professional" - this is cyberspace, baby!
